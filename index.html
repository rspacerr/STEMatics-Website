Mission Statement

A program to prepare curious minds to adapt to increasingly competitive demands through
an innovative disciplinary basic STEM environment.

About STEMatics

STEMatics aims to bring middle schoolers a high level education in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematical fields far beyond the scope of most middle schools. Students will not only get help on their homework (if they desire it) but will also gain access to primier education and access to begin personal projects and research. Students willing to partake can also participate in competitions against their peers and others in a range of fields.

About Us

Meet the Club Founders and Teachers
STEMatics is a club formed by students at Bergen County Academies, who, inspired by their own education, sought to bring valuable STEM based learning to younger children. Students are educated in various fields in STEM, having taken Honors and Advanced Placement level classes in all of the fields of STEM.
