  • Math at STEMatics

In STEMatics, the vast and mysterious fields of mathematics are made simple to navigate and can be tackled through many fun challenges and exercises. From help with algebraic and geometric coursework to applied problem solving using complicated math, we offer quality advice and help.

At STEMatics, we aim to provide a comprehensive and high level education to students in mathematics. Students will receive help on their homework and an introduction to higher level topics, including those featured in Competition Math. Subjects include Algebra, Geometry, Combinatorics, Trigonometry, and Calculus. Those interested will have the opportunity to enroll in competitions such as the AMC.
